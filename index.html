About Us

We are based in the heart of East London, covering all of East London and parts of Essex. No matter the size of the job, all our work is carried out with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.

We pride ourselves on our high standards of workmanship. Owned and operated by Mr. Charlie Staley, a City & Guilds Level 2 qualified plumber, we cover all aspects of plumbing, from small jobs like tap replacements to full bathroom installations.

With a perfect 10/10 rating based on 27 customer reviews, you can trust us for reliability, quality, and excellent communication.
